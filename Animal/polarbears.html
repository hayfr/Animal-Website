Polar Bears

Polar bears live in the Arctic and are great swimmers. They have white fur to blend in with snow and ice.

Fun Facts

  • They eat mostly seals.
  • They can swim for miles in cold water.
  • They are the largest bear species.

What Polar Bears Do

  1. They hunt seals on ice.
  2. They swim very long distances.
  3. They make dens out of snow.
