Black Bears

Black bears are smaller than brown bears and live mostly in forests.

Fun Facts

  • They are good at climbing trees.
  • They are found mostly in North America.
  • They eat berries, plants, and insects.

What Black Bears Do

  1. Are nocturnal in areas with human activities.
  2. Black bears can be brown and grey.
  3. More passive compared to brown and polar bears.
