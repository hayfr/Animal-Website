Brown Bears

Brown bears are large animals that live in forests, mountains, and tundras.

Wikipedia

Fun Facts

  • They can weigh up to 1,500 pounds.
  • They live in North America, Europe, and Asia.
  • They eat plants, fish, and small animals.

What Brown Bears Do

  1. Sleep during winter (hibernate)
  2. Look for food in spring and summer
  3. Take care of their cubs
